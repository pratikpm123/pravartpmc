Our Services

Project Management Consultancy

Pravart's primary goal is to ensure that their clients' construction projects are completed on time, within budget, and to the required standard of quality. We work closely with clients to identify their needs and goals, and then develop a project.

Architecture Design & Landscaping

We provide services for planning and architecture for housing and industrial, buildings and plants, interior and landscaping. We have in house as well as associated architects to look after planning and architecture.

Structure Design

We specialize in structural design for residential and industrial buildings, ensuring stability and safety. Our expertise also includes designing and assessing the strength of space frames with precision for reliable and robust structural solutions.

Quantity Surveying

Our company prepares accurate and detailed cost estimates and budgets for construction projects. We help clients in forecasting the costs of the project from the outset to the completion stage, to ensure that the project is delivered within the budget.

Cost Consultancy

We help customer by developing cost plans and cost controls for the project, monitoring and reviewing costs throughout the project lifecycle, and providing advice on how to manage and minimize project costs.

Techno Legal Services

Techno-legal services in our company help clients navigate the legal and technical complexities of construction projects, mitigate risks, and ensure compliance with applicable laws and regulations.

Why Choose Us

Pravart has a team of experienced construction professionals, including project managers, engineers, architects, quantity surveyors, and other specialists. We use a range of tools and techniques to manage the construction process, including project management software, risk assessment tools, and performance metrics.
